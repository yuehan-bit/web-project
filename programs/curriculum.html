Program Curriculum

The NROTC curriculum is designed to provide a balance of academic learning, leadership training, and physical fitness. The program includes:

  • Military Science Courses: Covers military tactics, strategy, and leadership principles.
  • Field Training Exercises: Hands-on training in a real-world environment.
  • Physical Fitness Training: Regular exercises to maintain physical readiness.
  • Leadership Seminars: Workshops and seminars to enhance leadership skills.
