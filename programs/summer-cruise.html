Summer Cruise Training

The Summer Cruise is an essential part of the NROTC experience, providing hands-on training in a real-world military environment. Activities include:

  • Shipboard operations
  • Leadership exercises
  • Navigation and seamanship
  • Interaction with active-duty personnel

These experiences help cadets gain practical knowledge and prepare for future leadership roles.
